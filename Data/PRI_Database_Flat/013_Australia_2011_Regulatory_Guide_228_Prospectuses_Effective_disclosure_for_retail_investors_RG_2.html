RG 228 Prospectuses: Effective disclosure for retail investors

regulatory guide 228

Issued 12 August 2019

This guide is for issuers and advisers. It explains how to word and present prospectuses and other documents in a clear, concise and effective manner.

This guide also sets out our guidance about how to prepare prospectuses that satisfy the content requirements in s710 of the Corporations Act.
